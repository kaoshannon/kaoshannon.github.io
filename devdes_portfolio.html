BYO

March 2021 - present

BYO is an app that encourages coffee and bubble tea drinkers to bring their own cup through a reward system. When users visit a participating cafe with a reuseable cup for their drink, they are provided a QR code unique to the cafe to scan and earn points. We plant a tree for every 5 cups you save, and you get to earn discounts with your points!

CanadaToGo/VanBCToGo

August 2020 - present

CanadaToGo (initiated in Vancouver exclusively under VanBCToGo) is a web-based listing platform for local restaurants Canada-wide. Driven to find a solution for the uncertainty caused by the COVID-19 pandemic, the CanadaToGo and VanBCToGo platforms are equipped with updated hours of operation, takeout options, and dietary preferences.

With additional features like the interactive maps and live capacity information for each of our locations, we promote the direct connection for people to restaurants, with a convenience that encourages consumers to contact and order their meals directly from the restaurant rather than from other services that cut into our local restaurants' profit margins, whether they are coming back to their comfort foods or discovering new places to dine at.

With much of the site constructed in PHP, our streamlined styling and functionalities prioritize the user accessibility. Furthermore, the live capacity feature is possible through a Python web-scraper that updates with real-time information automatically.

I joined the CanadaToGo team the summer prior to my freshman year at UBC with an initial concentration in debugging, frontend and backend design respectively, but quickly became involved with the holistic expansion of our platform and the data implementation through API web integration.

This student-propelled project aims to bring people together with the food they know and love, supporting our local dining businesses through and beyond the pandemic.

Explore: CanadaToGo, VanBCToGo








UBC BizTech

August 2020 - April 2021

As a design director with UBC BizTech, the bulk of my work is creating promotional material and web app design. From basic social media graphics, event branding, and customizing merchandise and prizes, to interactive media design and user interface design in collaboration with our internal development team, my interconnected role on the team has pushed me to hone my technical design skills and exposed me to opportunities for collaboration for every project.

With most events and conferences, I strive to capture the essence of the experience in the designs by being in a constant feedback loop with the event directors. Many of our events are branded a certain way already, so the importance of balancing between innovating and respecting past designers' visions is critical to making a good design.

On the other hand, when working on the web app, it's crucial to understand what's technically implementable behind each feature we are designing. Much of the time and communication is spent in the refinement process, where we reiterate several versions of each design to optimize user flow and streamline layout.

Explore: UBC BizTech, BizTech WebApp
